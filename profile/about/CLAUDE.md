## Editing Rules

- Any change to brand copy (mission, vision, values, slogan) must be confirmed with the user — these are strategic organizational decisions
- Keep the tone: clear, direct, aspirational, inclusive
- Do not add marketing jargon or buzzwords inconsistent with the existing tone
